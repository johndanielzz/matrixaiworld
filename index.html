FAQ

Common buying questions

How do I find parts compatible with my specific vehicle?

Use the "Find Parts for Your Vehicle" search at the top of this page. Select your make, model, year, and part type. You can also enter a keyword or OEM part number in the main search bar.

How do I compare products?

Click the "+ Compare" button on any product card. Select up to 3 products, then click "Compare Now" in the bar at the bottom of the screen to see a side-by-side specification table.

Can I place an order directly from this site?

Yes. The checkout creates a real order entry and lets you continue to WhatsApp or Facebook with the order reference to confirm payment and delivery details.

How fast will I get a quote response?

Most quotes are responded to within 30 minutes during business hours (8am–8pm). Urgent same-day requests are flagged and handled first.

Do promo codes actually apply discounts?

Yes. Enter your code on the checkout page to see the discount applied to your total before you confirm with the sales team.

Will my cart and wishlist be saved if I close the browser?

Yes. All data (cart, wishlist, orders, quotes) is saved to Firebase so it persists across sessions and devices.
