# 📱 Mat Auto App Installation Guide

Your Mat Auto store is now a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** that works on all devices!

## Installation Instructions

### ✅ Android (Phone & Tablet)

1. **Via Install Prompt:**
   - Open the site in Chrome, Edge, or Firefox
   - You'll see an "Install Mat Auto" popup at the bottom
   - Tap **Install** → The app adds to your home screen

2. **Manual Installation:**
   - Open the site in your browser
   - Tap the **⋮ (menu)** button → **Install app**
   - Or: **⋮ → Add to Home screen**

3. **From App Shelf:**
   - Some Android versions show an app icon in the toolbar
   - Tap it to install

---

### 🍎 iPhone & iPad (iOS)

1. Open the site in **Safari** (important!)
2. Tap the **Share** button (arrow box at bottom)
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Name it "Mat Auto" and tap **Add**
5. The app now appears on your home screen

**Note:** iOS apps work similarly to web apps - they're not in the App Store yet but function identically.

---

### 🖥️ Windows & Mac (Desktop)

1. Open the site in **Chrome**, **Edge**, or **Firefox**
2. Look for the **install icon** in the address bar (⬇️ or ⌗)
3. Click it and confirm
4. The app adds to your Start Menu / Applications

### ⚙️ Troubleshooting

**Not seeing install prompt?**
- Refresh the page
- Make sure you're using Chrome, Edge, or Firefox (Safari needs manual install)
- Check that site is loaded over HTTPS (when hosted live)

**App won't load offline?**
- Close and reopen the app
- Make sure you visited the site first (cache builds on first visit)

**Update the app?**
- Quit and reopen the app
- Updates happen automatically
