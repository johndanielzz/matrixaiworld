🔄 How to Return or Claim Warranty

1

Contact Our Team

Message us on WhatsApp or via the Contact page within the warranty period. Include your Order ID and a brief description of the issue.

2

Send Photos or Video

Send clear photos or a short video of the defective part via WhatsApp. This helps us assess the issue quickly and determine the fastest resolution.

3

Assessment (24–48 hours)

Our technical team will review your claim. For straightforward defects, we approve immediately. For complex cases, an in-person inspection may be arranged at no cost to you.

4

Return the Item

Once approved, return the item to our location or arrange a pickup (we may cover pickup costs for approved warranty claims). Pack the item securely in its original packaging if possible.

5

Replacement or Refund

We'll send a replacement part immediately (subject to stock availability) or process a full refund within 3–5 business days via your original payment method.

⚠️ What Is NOT Covered

The following are excluded from all Mat Auto warranty coverage:

  • Damage caused by improper installation or modification
  • Normal wear and tear (brake pad thinning, filter clogging, etc.)
  • Damage from accidents, misuse, or neglect
  • Parts installed in vehicles with pre-existing engine damage
  • Damage from incorrect fluids, overheating, or flooding
  • Products with tampered or removed serial numbers
  • Claims made after the warranty period has expired
  • Labor costs for installation or removal

💡 Tips to Protect Your Warranty

📸 Document Installation

Take photos before and during installation. This protects you if a warranty claim is ever questioned.

🔧 Use a Certified Mechanic

Have parts installed by a qualified mechanic. Improper installation voids the warranty.

🧾 Keep Your Receipt

Save your Order ID and WhatsApp confirmation. You'll need these to make a warranty claim.

📦 Keep Packaging

Retain the original packaging for at least 30 days in case a return is needed.
